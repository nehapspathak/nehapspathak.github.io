Introduction

My name is Kumari Neha. I am a Ph.D. Research Scholar at IIIT Delhi, India. I work in the field of Computational Social Science. My research topic is Understanding Protest Participation on Social Media. I have worked on understanding people's participation in Indian Protests, including Citizenship Amendment Act, Farmer's Protest, and Shushan Singh Rajput's death row.
